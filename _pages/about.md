Dr. Hongxun Hui is an Assistant Professor with the State Key Laboratory of Internet of Things for Smart City, University of Macau. Previously, he received a B. Eng. degree in 2015 and Ph.D. degree in 2020 both from the College of Electrical Engineering in Zhejiang University. From 2018 to 2019, he was a visiting scholar at the Advanced Research Institute in Virginia Tech and CURENT Center in University of Tennessee. His research focuses on smart grid optimization and control, demand response, power economics, carbon markets, and interdisciplinary energy-environment systems. He has published 1 international book and more than 40 SCI journal papers as the first/corresponding author. Four of the papers was selected as the ESI Highly Cited Papers (Top 1%). He has led 7 competitive projects as Principal Investigator (PI), including grants from the National Natural Science Foundation of China (NSFC), the National Key R&D Program of China (sub-project), the Macao Science and Technology Development Fund (FDCT), and Guangdong Basic and Applied Basic Research Foundation.

Dr. Hongxun Hui is Young Editorial Board Member and Guest Editor of high-level journals, including Research, The Innovation Energy, Applied Energy, Protection and Control of Modern Power Systems, Energy Conversion and Economics, Engineering Reports, Integrated Intelligent Energy, etc. He was elected in the 1st batch of the Academic Rising Star Program for Ph.D. at Zhejiang University in 2018. Besides, he got the Outstanding Reviewer Award from the Journal of Modern Power Systems and Clean Energy in 2018, the Best Paper Award of IEEE iSPEC-2021, the First Prize and the Best Innovation Award of the 2021 National AI Application Competition in Power Dispatching, the Excellent Paper Award of the Integrated Smart Energy Conference in 2023, the Best Paper Award of IEEE EI2-2023, and the Excellent Peer Reviewer Award from CSEE Journal of Power and Energy Systems in 2024. He was elected as Young Elite Scientists Sponsorship Program by CSEE in 2024.
